A Lonely Bean

A lonely Bean game

This is a 3D platformer where you have to collect all the beans and make it to the end portal to win! Make sure to collect all beans 9 though so you don't get a bad ending! Falling won't end well also so be careful!

Pet Collect

Pet collect in scratch

This is a clicker game where you could earn money to buy eggs to hatch pets! You could get pets that vary in rarity from common to legendary! These pets also give you boosts to gain more money! You could also buy power ups to help you! The goal is to collect every pet!

Drawing App

Drawing App in Snap

A fun drawing app where you could create amazing drawings! This includes a paint tool, an eraser tool, and color sliders! Though we are still working on this app, it's still a great way to show off your artistic skills!
